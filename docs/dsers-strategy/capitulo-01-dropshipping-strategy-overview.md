# PURPOSE

This document defines how Bliss Glow operates dropshipping with
DSers and AliExpress for the United Kingdom market.

It covers supplier selection, product research, pricing,
fulfilment, shipping, returns, compliance and scaling.

------------------------------------------------------------

# BUSINESS MODEL

Customer buys on blissglow.store (prices in GBP, VAT inclusive)

Order syncs to DSers automatically

DSers places the order with the AliExpress supplier

Supplier ships directly to the customer in the UK

Bliss Glow never holds physical stock

------------------------------------------------------------

# WHY THIS MODEL

No upfront inventory investment

Wide catalogue testing at low risk

Focus capital on marketing and brand

Clear path to private label once winners are proven

------------------------------------------------------------

# SUCCESS CRITERIA

Delivery to UK customer within 10 days door-to-door

Gross margin at or above 60% after all costs

Dispute rate under 2% of orders
