# PURPOSE

Sell into the UK legally and without surprise costs for
customers.

------------------------------------------------------------

# VAT

UK VAT applies to consignments; for imports 135 GBP or less,
VAT is collected at the point of sale

AliExpress/supplier lines used must be delivered duty paid —
the customer must NEVER face a customs bill

Store prices are always VAT-inclusive

------------------------------------------------------------

# PRODUCT COMPLIANCE

Electricals (LED masks, steamers): UKCA/CE marking required

Cosmetics that touch skin: check UK cosmetic regulations
(responsible person, ingredient labelling) before listing

Batteries: supplier must use compliant shipping lines

------------------------------------------------------------

# CONSUMER LAW

Consumer Rights Act 2015: 30-day right to reject faulty goods

Consumer Contracts Regulations: 14-day cancellation right —
our 30-day policy exceeds it

CMA pricing guidance: genuine "was" prices only

------------------------------------------------------------

# RECORD KEEPING

Keep order, VAT and refund records for HMRC (6 years).
