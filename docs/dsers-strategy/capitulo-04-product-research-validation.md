# PURPOSE

Systematically find and validate products worthy of the
Bliss Glow catalogue.

------------------------------------------------------------

# RESEARCH SOURCES

AliExpress Dropshipping Center (top performing beauty items)

DSers Supplier Optimizer

TikTok / Instagram beauty trends (UK creators)

Competitor UK stores and Amazon UK best sellers

Customer requests and chat questions

------------------------------------------------------------

# VALIDATION CHECKLIST

Solves a clear beauty or wellness need

Sells at 3x or more of landed cost

Lightweight, robust, low breakage risk

No batteries or liquids restrictions issues for UK import
(or supplier handles compliance)

Demonstrable in short video (marketing potential)

Not dominated by a single strong UK brand

------------------------------------------------------------

# TEST PROTOCOL

Order a sample to a UK address before listing

Inspect quality, packaging and true delivery time

Photograph the real product for the store

Only then: import, price and publish
