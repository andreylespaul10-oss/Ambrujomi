# PURPOSE

Choose suppliers that protect the premium Bliss Glow
experience.

------------------------------------------------------------

# MANDATORY CRITERIA

Supplier rating 4.6 or higher

At least 500 orders on the listing

Positive feedback 96% or higher

Ships from a warehouse with UK-viable delivery
(UK warehouse preferred; otherwise AliExpress Standard
Shipping to the UK in 10 days or less)

Processing time 2 days or less

Responsive to messages within 24 hours

------------------------------------------------------------

# PREFERRED CRITERIA

European or UK local warehouse

Branded or neutral packaging (no invoices in the box)

Experience with dropshipping (accepts "do not include
promotions" note)

Offers the same product in multiple colour variants

------------------------------------------------------------

# RED FLAGS

Price far below every competitor (quality risk)

New store with few reviews

Photos stolen from other listings

Inconsistent variant naming

No response to pre-sale questions
