# PURPOSE

Measure the dropshipping operation with a weekly scorecard.

------------------------------------------------------------

# WEEKLY SCORECARD

Orders and revenue (GBP)

Gross margin % (after product, shipping, fees, refunds)

Average time: payment -> supplier order placed

Average time: payment -> tracking uploaded

Average door-to-door delivery days

Refund rate % and dispute rate %

Dispute recovery rate (money back from suppliers)

Top 10 products by margin (not only by revenue)

Supplier scorecard averages (Ch. 12)

------------------------------------------------------------

# TARGETS

Margin 60%+ | Tracking within 72h: 95%+ |
Delivery 10 days or less: 90%+ | Refunds under 3% |
Disputes under 2%

------------------------------------------------------------

# REVIEW CADENCE

Weekly: operations scorecard

Monthly: supplier scorecards, product retirement list,
pricing review

Quarterly: strategy review — scaling stage, private label
candidates
