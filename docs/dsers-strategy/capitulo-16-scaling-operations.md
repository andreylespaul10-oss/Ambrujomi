# PURPOSE

Grow from first sales to hundreds of orders per day without
breaking the operation.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SCALING STAGES

Stage 1 (0-10 orders/day): founder-operated, daily DSers
routine (Ch. 7)

Stage 2 (10-50/day): part-time VA for fulfilment + support
macros + Level 2/3 suppliers on winners

Stage 3 (50-200/day): dropshipping agent or 3PL for winners
(bulk stock, UK warehouse, 2-day delivery), DSers remains
for long-tail catalogue

Stage 4 (200+/day): private label winners, UK fulfilment
centre, DSers only for testing new products

------------------------------------------------------------

# WHEN TO MOVE A PRODUCT OFF ALIEXPRESS

Consistent 10+ orders/day for 3 weeks

Margin improves 15%+ via agent/bulk

Delivery time improves to 2-3 days -> better reviews,
lower refunds
