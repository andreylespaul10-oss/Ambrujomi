# PURPOSE

Turn good suppliers into partners for better prices,
speed and reliability.

------------------------------------------------------------

# RELATIONSHIP LADDER

Level 1 — Transactional: standard listings, no contact

Level 2 — Regular: weekly orders; ask for dropshipping
support, faster processing, neutral packaging

Level 3 — Partner: negotiated prices, dedicated stock,
branded inserts, direct WhatsApp/WeChat line

Level 4 — Private label: custom packaging with Bliss Glow
branding, minimum order quantities, quality contract

------------------------------------------------------------

# NEGOTIATION TRIGGERS

30+ orders/month with one supplier -> ask for 5-10% price cut

100+ orders/month -> negotiate dedicated stock + branding

------------------------------------------------------------

# SUPPLIER SCORECARD (MONTHLY)

Processing speed | Tracking validity | Defect rate |
Dispute outcomes | Communication — rated 1-5;
under 3 average = find replacement
