# PURPOSE

Deliver a premium "free delivery" promise on top of
AliExpress logistics.

------------------------------------------------------------

# CUSTOMER PROMISE

FREE UK delivery on every order

Stated delivery window: 2-4 working days for UK-warehouse
items; up to 7-10 days otherwise

Tracking link for every parcel

------------------------------------------------------------

# SUPPLIER SHIPPING RULES

Prefer suppliers with UK warehouses (true 2-4 day delivery)

Otherwise AliExpress Standard Shipping (tracked) —
never untracked economy lines

Never use lines with customs risk for the customer
(delivered duty unpaid is forbidden)

------------------------------------------------------------

# CARRIERS SEEN IN THE UK

Royal Mail, Evri, Yodel, DPD (last mile after AliExpress
Standard Shipping handoff)

------------------------------------------------------------

# HONESTY RULE

The product page must show the realistic delivery window
for THAT product's supplier. Under-promise, over-deliver.
