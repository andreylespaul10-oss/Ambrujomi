# Version 1.0
# ============================================================

# PURPOSE

Move every paid order to "shipped with tracking" quickly
and without errors.

------------------------------------------------------------

# STANDARD FLOW

Order paid on store -> appears in DSers "Awaiting Order"

Operator reviews (address complete? risk flags?)

Bulk place orders to AliExpress (once daily minimum)

Pay in one batch

Supplier ships (target: within 48h)

Tracking auto-syncs to store -> customer email sent

------------------------------------------------------------

# ORDER NOTES TO SUPPLIER

Every order includes the note:
"Dropshipping order — please do NOT include invoices,
promotions or QR codes in the package. Thank you."

------------------------------------------------------------

# SLA TARGETS

Order placed with supplier: within 24h of payment

Tracking uploaded: within 72h of payment

Customer receives tracking email automatically

------------------------------------------------------------

# EXCEPTIONS

Address problems -> contact customer same day via email/chat

Supplier does not ship within 3 days -> open DSers chat,
then switch to backup supplier and cancel original
