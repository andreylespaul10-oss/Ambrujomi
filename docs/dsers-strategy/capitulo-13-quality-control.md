# PURPOSE

Protect the premium brand promise with systematic quality
checks.

------------------------------------------------------------

# CONTROLS

Sample order for every new product (Chapter 4)

Re-sample every 6 months or after supplier change

Monitor review ratings per product weekly —
under 4.2 average triggers investigation

Defect log per product and per supplier

Mystery-shop own store quarterly (full order experience)

------------------------------------------------------------

# ACTION THRESHOLDS

Defect rate above 3% -> switch supplier or delist

Repeated packaging damage -> require better packaging
or change supplier

Any safety concern (heat products, electricals) ->
immediate delist + review compliance
