# PURPOSE

Price for premium positioning, free delivery and healthy
margin.

------------------------------------------------------------

# PRICING FORMULA

Landed cost = product cost + supplier shipping to UK
+ payment fees (~2.9% + 20p) + expected refund allowance (2%)

Selling price = landed cost x 3 (minimum x2.5)
rounded to psychological .99 endings

All prices include VAT and FREE UK delivery
(shipping cost is embedded in the product price)

------------------------------------------------------------

# EXAMPLES

Product cost 4.50 + shipping 2.80 = landed ~7.80
-> sell at 19.99 or above

Product cost 12.00 + shipping 4.00 = landed ~16.70
-> sell at 39.99 or above

------------------------------------------------------------

# DISCOUNT RULES

Coupons (GLOW10, WELCOME15) must keep net margin above 50%.

"Was" prices reflect genuine previous prices (UK CMA
compliance — no fake anchoring).

Never discount below 2x landed cost.
