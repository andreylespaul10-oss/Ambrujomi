# PURPOSE

Prevent selling what suppliers cannot deliver.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SYNC RULES

DSers stock and price alerts checked daily

Supplier out of stock -> switch to backup supplier mapping
OR hide product from store within 24 hours

Supplier price increase above 10% -> review selling price
before accepting new orders

------------------------------------------------------------

# OPERATING ROUTINE

Daily (10 min): DSers notifications, failed orders,
stock alerts

Weekly: margin check on top 10 sellers, supplier message
follow-ups

Monthly: full catalogue stock audit, retire slow movers
