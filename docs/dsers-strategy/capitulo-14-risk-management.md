# PURPOSE

Anticipate the failure modes of dropshipping and have a
playbook ready.

------------------------------------------------------------

# TOP RISKS & PLAYBOOKS

Supplier stockout -> backup supplier mapped in DSers (Ch. 5)

Shipping delays (Chinese New Year, Nov-Dec peak) ->
banner with adjusted delivery estimates + order buffer stock
with Level 3 suppliers before peak

Supplier raises price silently -> DSers price alerts + weekly
margin review

Account risk (AliExpress/DSers suspension) -> keep supplier
contacts off-platform for continuity

Payment disputes/chargebacks -> tracking evidence submitted
via Stripe; ship only to verified addresses on high-value
orders

Currency swings (USD cost vs GBP revenue) -> reprice when
FX moves over 5%

------------------------------------------------------------

# SEASONAL CALENDAR

Chinese New Year (Jan/Feb): suppliers close ~2 weeks — warn
customers, pre-arrange stock

Q4 peak: longer lines, raise delivery estimates on pages
