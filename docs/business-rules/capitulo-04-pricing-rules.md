# CHAPTER 4
# PRICING RULES
# ============================================================

# PURPOSE

Define how product prices are calculated, displayed and maintained
across the Bliss Glow platform.

------------------------------------------------------------

# PRICE TYPES

Regular Price

Sale Price

Promotional Price

Bundle Price

Member Price

------------------------------------------------------------

# PRICING RULES

Prices displayed in GBP (£)

VAT included where legally required

Only one active sale price per product

Sale period must have start and end dates

Historical prices retained for auditing

------------------------------------------------------------

# VALIDATION

Price cannot be negative

Sale price must be lower than regular price

Currency consistency required
