# PURPOSE

Make every AI feature improve automatically and safely
over time.

------------------------------------------------------------

# FEEDBACK LOOPS

Recommendation clicks and purchases retrain ranking weekly

Search reformulations teach synonyms and spelling

Chatbot handovers become training examples after human review

Quiz outcomes refine the skincare matching rules

------------------------------------------------------------

# RETRAINING POLICY

Scheduled: monthly for ranking models, quarterly for
prediction models.

Triggered: drift alerts or KPI drops trigger early retraining.

Every retrained model passes validation before deployment
(see Chapter 16).

------------------------------------------------------------

# HUMAN IN THE LOOP

The merchandising team can pin, boost or exclude products
from AI surfaces; manual rules always win over model output.
