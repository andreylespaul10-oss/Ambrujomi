# PURPOSE

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence shall be designed,
built and operated across the Bliss Glow platform.

AI must serve three goals:

• Increase conversion and average order value
• Reduce operational cost and manual work
• Improve the customer experience at every touchpoint

------------------------------------------------------------

# AI VISION

Bliss Glow shall feel like a personal beauty advisor:
every customer sees a shop curated for her skin, her routine
and her preferences — automatically.

------------------------------------------------------------

# GUIDING PRINCIPLES

Customer benefit first

Measurable business impact

Privacy by design (UK GDPR)

Human oversight for sensitive decisions

Start simple, evolve with data volume

------------------------------------------------------------

# PHASED ROLLOUT

Phase 1 — Launch
• Rule-based recommendations (best sellers, related products)
• AI chatbot for support (FAQ, order tracking)

Phase 2 — Growth
• Behaviour-based recommendations
• Personalised email content
• Predictive stock alerts

Phase 3 — Enterprise
• Real-time personalisation engine
• Skincare AI assistant
• Dynamic pricing intelligence

------------------------------------------------------------

# BUSINESS OBJECTIVES

Recommendation revenue share: 15%+ of sales

Chatbot resolution rate: 70%+ without human help

Personalised email CTR: 2x generic campaigns
