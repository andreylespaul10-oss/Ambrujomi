# PURPOSE

Use AI to increase marketing efficiency and revenue per
customer across email, campaigns and offers.

------------------------------------------------------------

# CAPABILITIES

Email Personalisation — product blocks per recipient

Send-Time Optimisation — deliver when each customer opens

Subject Line Optimisation — A/B tested variants

Campaign Optimisation — budget shifts to winning audiences

Segmentation — automatic clusters (new, active, at-risk, VIP)

Offer Optimisation — right discount depth per segment

------------------------------------------------------------

# AUTOMATED JOURNEYS

Welcome series (WELCOME15)

Abandoned basket (1h, 24h, 72h)

Post-purchase care and cross-sell

Replenishment reminders (consumables: serums, patches)

Win-back for lapsed customers

------------------------------------------------------------

# BUSINESS RULES

Frequency cap: maximum 3 marketing emails per week per customer.

All AI-generated copy is reviewed before a new template goes live.

Consent (UK GDPR / PECR) is enforced at send time, every time.
