# PURPOSE

Anticipate customer behaviour and demand so the business acts
before problems and opportunities materialise.

------------------------------------------------------------

# PREDICTION MODELS

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
• Expected revenue per customer over 24 months

Purchase Prediction
• Probability of purchase in the next 30 days

Churn Prediction
• Customers at risk of not returning

Demand Forecast
• Units per product per week, seasonality-aware

Inventory Forecast
• Stockout risk and reorder timing per supplier lead time

------------------------------------------------------------

# ACTIONS FROM PREDICTIONS

High CLV -> VIP treatment and early access

Churn risk -> win-back journey triggers automatically

Demand spike -> supplier order suggestion in admin panel

Stockout risk -> raise product priority with DSers supplier

------------------------------------------------------------

# BUSINESS RULES

Predictions inform decisions; humans approve supplier orders.

Model accuracy is reviewed monthly against actuals.
