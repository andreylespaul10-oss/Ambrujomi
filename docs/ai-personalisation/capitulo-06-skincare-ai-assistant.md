# PURPOSE

A guided skincare advisor that builds personalised routines
and recommends compatible products — the signature premium
feature of Bliss Glow.

------------------------------------------------------------

# CUSTOMER FLOW

Skin Quiz (2 minutes)
• Skin type, concerns, sensitivities, current routine, goals

AI Analysis
• Maps answers to concern profiles and ingredient needs

Routine Builder
• Morning routine and night routine, step by step

Product Matching
• Bliss Glow products per step, with compatibility notes

Follow-Up
• Replenishment reminders and progress check-ins by email

------------------------------------------------------------

# SAFETY RULES

The assistant gives cosmetic guidance only — never medical advice.

Users reporting medical skin conditions are advised to consult a GP
or dermatologist.

Ingredient conflict warnings (e.g. retinol + strong exfoliants)
are always displayed.

# KPIS

Quiz completion rate

Quiz-to-purchase conversion
