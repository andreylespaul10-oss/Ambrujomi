# PURPOSE

Define the single source of truth for measuring AI impact.

------------------------------------------------------------

# NORTH STAR METRICS

AI-attributed revenue share — % of revenue from AI surfaces
(recommendations, search, personalised email)

Target: 25% by end of Phase 2

------------------------------------------------------------

# FEATURE METRICS

Recommendations: CTR, attach rate, revenue per session

Search: conversion, zero-result rate, autocomplete usage

Chatbot: resolution rate, CSAT, cost per conversation

Personalisation: uplift vs non-personalised control group

Predictions: forecast accuracy, churn-save rate

Fraud: fraud loss rate, false-positive rate

------------------------------------------------------------

# MEASUREMENT RULES

Every AI feature launches with a holdout control group.

Uplift is reported against control, not against zero.

Metrics are reviewed monthly in the AI review meeting.
