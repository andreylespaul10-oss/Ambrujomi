# PURPOSE

Summarise the standards every current and future AI feature
must meet.

------------------------------------------------------------

# THE BLISS GLOW AI STANDARD

Every AI feature must be:

Useful — measurable customer or business benefit

Ethical — transparent, fair, never manipulative

Secure — protected against abuse and data leakage

Compliant — UK GDPR, PECR and Article 22 respected

Observable — monitored with alerts and dashboards

Reversible — one-action rollback, manual override

Accountable — named owner and quarterly review

------------------------------------------------------------

# FUTURE ROADMAP

Visual search (photo -> similar products)

AR try-on for select tools

Voice commerce readiness

AI-generated product photography review workflow

Multi-language personalisation for international expansion

------------------------------------------------------------

# CLOSING PRINCIPLE

Artificial Intelligence shall support customer experience,
operational excellence and business growth while remaining
ethical, transparent, secure and compliant.
