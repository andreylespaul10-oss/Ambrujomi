# PURPOSE

Ensure every AI feature is responsible, explainable and
accountable.

------------------------------------------------------------

# GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK

Responsible AI — AI benefits the customer, never manipulates

Bias Detection — recommendations audited for unfair skew

Explainability — every AI decision affecting a customer can
be explained in plain language

Human Review — sensitive decisions (fraud holds, big price
changes) always include a human

Audit Logs — AI decisions logged with inputs and model version

------------------------------------------------------------

# ACCOUNTABILITY

Every AI feature has a named owner.

Quarterly AI review: performance, incidents, bias checks.

Customers may request human review of automated decisions
(UK GDPR Article 22).
