# PURPOSE

Keep the right products available without over-committing
capital — critical in dropshipping where stock is remote.

------------------------------------------------------------

# CAPABILITIES

Demand Prediction — expected sales per product per week

Stock Optimisation — flags slow movers and rising stars

Supplier Forecasting — accounts for AliExpress lead times

Safety Stock — buffer rules per product velocity tier

------------------------------------------------------------

# DROPSHIPPING RULES

Supplier stock is checked via DSers sync at least daily.

Products whose supplier stock falls below the 7-day demand
forecast are flagged "at risk" in the admin panel.

Supplier out-of-stock automatically hides the product
from recommendations (never from direct URL).

------------------------------------------------------------

# KPIS

Stockout hours per month

Oversell incidents (target: zero)

Forecast accuracy vs actual sales
