# PURPOSE

Make product discovery effortless with intelligent,
forgiving and fast search.

------------------------------------------------------------

# CAPABILITIES

Natural Language Search — "something for puffy eyes"

Autocomplete — suggestions after 2 characters, under 100 ms

Spell Correction — "guasha" finds "Gua Sha"

Synonyms — "face roller" = "facial roller" = "jade roller"

Semantic Search — matches intent, not only keywords

Intent Detection — product vs category vs help question

------------------------------------------------------------

# RANKING SIGNALS

Text relevance

Sales velocity

Stock availability

Margin (as a tie-breaker only)

Personal affinity (viewed categories)

------------------------------------------------------------

# BUSINESS RULES

Zero-results pages must show alternatives, never a dead end.

Out-of-stock products rank last, never first.

Search analytics feed the merchandising team weekly.

------------------------------------------------------------

# KPIS

Search-to-purchase conversion

Zero-result rate (target under 3%)

Autocomplete usage rate
