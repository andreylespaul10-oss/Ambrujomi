## PURPOSE

This document defines the Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) strategy for
the Bliss Glow e-commerce platform.

The objective is to maximise qualified organic traffic, improve search
visibility, increase conversion rates and establish Bliss Glow as a
trusted authority in the premium beauty and skincare market within the
United Kingdom.
