# ============================================================
# PART 5 — HOME PAGE SPECIFICATION
# Bliss Glow
# ============================================================

# HOME PAGE OBJECTIVE

The homepage is the most important page of the website.

Its mission is to:

• Build trust immediately.
• Present the Bliss Glow brand.
• Showcase featured collections.
• Highlight promotions.
• Guide visitors to products quickly.
• Increase conversion rates.
• Encourage exploration.

Every section should naturally lead the customer toward making a purchase.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 1 — Announcement Bar

Located at the very top.

Examples:

• Free Shipping on eligible orders.
• Seasonal Discounts.
• New Collection Available.
• Limited-Time Offers.

Must support rotation of multiple announcements.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 2 — Sticky Header

Contains:

• Logo
• Navigation Menu
• Search
• Wishlist
• Cart
• My Account
• Mobile Menu

Behavior:

• Transparent over the hero section.
• Becomes solid after scrolling.
• Always accessible.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 3 — Hero Section

This is the first impression of the brand.

Must include:

• Full-width immersive banner.
• Premium lifestyle photography.
• Elegant headline.
• Supporting description.
• Primary Call-to-Action ("Shop Now").
• Secondary Call-to-Action ("Explore Collection").

Support:

• Static images.
• Video background (optional).
• Smooth fade transitions.
• Mobile-optimized layouts.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 4 — Brand Benefits

Display the main reasons to shop with Bliss Glow.

Example benefits:

• Premium Quality
• Fast Shipping
• Secure Checkout
• Easy Returns
• Trusted Customer Support

Use icons and concise descriptions.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 5 — Featured Categories

Show visually engaging category cards.

Each card contains:

• Large image.
• Category name.
• Short description.
• Call-to-action.

Cards should animate subtly on hover.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 6 — Best Sellers

Carousel or responsive product grid.

Each product card displays:

• Product image.
• Product name.
• Price.
• Rating.
• Wishlist button.
• Quick View.
• Add to Cart.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 7 — New Arrivals

Highlight recently added products.

Support:

• Product labels.
• Horizontal carousel.
• Responsive behavior.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 8 — Promotional Banner

Large promotional section.

May include:

• Limited Offers.
• Holiday Campaigns.
• Coupon Promotions.
• Countdown Timer (optional).

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 9 — Customer Reviews

Display real customer testimonials.

Include:

• Customer name.
• Rating.
• Review text.
• Product purchased (optional).
• Customer image (optional).

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 10 — Featured Collection

Curated product collection.

Examples:

• Summer Collection.
• Self-Care Essentials.
• Best Gift Ideas.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 11 — Newsletter

Encourage visitors to subscribe.

Include:

• Email field.
• CTA button.
• Privacy notice.
• Success message after subscription.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 12 — Instagram / Social Gallery

Display lifestyle content.

Support image grid or carousel.

Encourage users to follow the brand.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 13 — Blog Preview

Show latest blog posts.

Each card includes:

• Featured image.
• Title.
• Short excerpt.
• Read More button.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 14 — Frequently Asked Questions

Expandable accordion.

Examples:

• Shipping.
• Returns.
• Payments.
• Product Information.
• Customer Support.

------------------------------------------------------------

# SECTION 15 — Footer

Must include:

• Company information.
• Customer support links.
• Shop links.
• Legal pages.
• Social media.
• Newsletter.
• Payment method icons.
• Copyright.

------------------------------------------------------------

# HOME PAGE PERFORMANCE

Requirements:

• Optimized images.
• Lazy loading.
• Responsive layout.
• Accessible navigation.
• Fast rendering.
• SEO metadata.
• Structured data.
• High Lighthouse score.

------------------------------------------------------------

# HOME PAGE UX PRINCIPLES

The page should:

• Feel luxurious without being overwhelming.
• Keep navigation simple.
• Minimize unnecessary clicks.
• Guide users toward featured products.
• Reinforce trust through social proof and clear information.

------------------------------------------------------------

# INTERACTIVE PROTOTYPE REQUIREMENTS

The generated prototype must allow users to click through every interactive element on the homepage.

Working interactions include:

• Navigation menu.
• Category cards.
• Product cards.
• Wishlist.
• Search.
• Shopping cart.
• Hero buttons.
• Promotional banners.
• Blog links.
• Footer links.

All interactions should lead to their respective pages, creating a realistic browsing experience.

End of Part 5.
