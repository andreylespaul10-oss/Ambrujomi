# ============================================================

# PURPOSE

The Bliss Glow Brand Identity defines how the brand should be presented
across every customer touchpoint.

The objective is to create a premium, memorable and trustworthy brand
that reflects modern beauty, confidence and wellbeing.

Every visual and written element must reinforce a consistent identity.

------------------------------------------------------------

# BRAND MISSION

To make premium beauty and skincare products accessible through an
exceptional online shopping experience built on trust, quality and
personalisation.

------------------------------------------------------------

# BRAND VISION

To become one of the United Kingdom's most trusted online destinations
for premium beauty and skincare products.

------------------------------------------------------------

# BRAND VALUES

Quality

Only trusted brands and carefully selected products.

Customer First

Every decision should improve the customer experience.

Transparency

Clear pricing, honest product information and straightforward policies.

Innovation

Continuous improvement through technology and AI.

Sustainability

Responsible packaging, ethical sourcing and environmentally conscious
business practices wherever possible.

Accessibility

Inclusive experiences designed for every customer.

# LOGO SYSTEM

Primary Logo

Full Bliss Glow wordmark.

Use on light backgrounds whenever possible.

Secondary Logo

Stacked version for compact layouts.

Icon Logo

Simplified "BG" mark for favicons, mobile applications and social media.

Monochrome Logo

Black or white version for limited colour applications.

------------------------------------------------------------

# LOGO USAGE

Always maintain visual clarity.

Never stretch the logo.

Never rotate the logo.

Never apply shadows.

Never distort proportions.

Never change official colours.

Never place the logo on visually busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast.

------------------------------------------------------------

# CLEAR SPACE

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of
the letter "B" in the wordmark.

No text or graphical elements may enter this protected area.
